== Description ==
                            
Meet Tidio Form, the easiest way to publish forms on your website. Beautifully designed, adapted to mobile devices, and thanks to integration with WordPress’s WYSWIG editor, it enables easy placement of forms in the content of a subpage.

[youtube https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xz07L_qxqnY]

= Functionalities: =

* Beautiful design 
* After the user fills out the form, the content is sent to your e-mail address 
* Anti-spam protection 
* Adapted to mobile devices 
* Messages are sent to your e-mail address or stored in the database 
* Support for servers that don’t support SMTP 
* Completely free!

== Installation ==
1. Go to WordPress Control Panel
1. Click "Plugins", then "Add New"
1. Type in "Tidio Form" and click "Search Plugins"
1. Download and install the Plugin
1. Click the "Activate Plugin" link
1. Move to the "Tidio Form" section in the menu
1. Done! Now you can start talk with your users

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How will the form contents be sent to my e-mail address?  =
If your server supports sending e-mails, messages will be sent directly from your server. If not, messages will be sent through our external API.

= Does the plugin have any hidden costs?  =
No, it’s completely free.

= How can I add the form while adding a post? =
Simply click the “Add Form�? button and a dialog box will open up from which you can choose a form to add on your page.
